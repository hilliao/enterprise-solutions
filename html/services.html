About us

consulting services

We partner with Google to deliver cutting edge technology consulting and professional services to small and mid size businesses. Our clients trust us to help them solve the most challenging problems in running their day to day IT operations. Our teams have veterans with experience migrating large enterprise scale workloads from data centers to Google cloud platform. In today’s competitive corporate landscape, poorly managed IT is often the cause of high expenses, delay to enter the market, failure to meet financial targets. With the power of Google cloud platform and highly skilled Google cloud experts, we can help you win the competition.

Infrastructure modernization and application migration

Legacy infrastructure and applications are one of the top issues modern IT operations are facing. Businesses are often running slow because IT can’t scale fast enough to meet demand. How can your IT scale up to meet the business peak demand in real time and scale down to save on infrastructure cost during off seasons? We have the answers to many common problems S&P 500 companies are facing around infrastructure and applications. Whether it’s VMs, data warehouse, microservices, databases, our teams have done large migrations and modernizations at many S&P 500 companies.

Data engineering, Analytics, Machine learning

Businesses make informed decisions based on data. In today’s world, every company needs to store, process, analyze, and derive intelligence from data it possesses. Poor data engineering leads to poor business decision making, then to poor financial performance. How can your sales department predict how many products will be sold in the next quarter given a product ID? How can you decide which customer to offer discounts? How can you build a data warehouse to derive intelligence around which product to recommend in the marketing campaign? We have the answers to the questions by leveraging Google cloud’s best data products and services including BigQuery ML, Vertex AI, Dataflow to solve the most technically challenging problems today’s businesses are facing.

Application development and System integration

We focus on cloud native development, continuous integration, continuous deployment on Google cloud. Our teams have the right skills to advise on the best solutions to many of the business problems and gaps you are facing. The key here is to identify the right software and services to leverage and integrate it to your business processes. We have expertise in building enterprise scale applications and services which entail security, reliability, availability, scalability, observability by following Google SRE best practices.
